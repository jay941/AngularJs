Mango ice cream

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Ripe Mango pureed .
  • 3 cups Milk.
  • 2 cups fresh cream.
  • 1 tin NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk .

Time Required

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Yield: Makes 1-quart

PREPARATION

1. Combine the NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk, milk and mango puree in a bowl. Whip the cream lightly and fold into the mixture..

2. Pour into a freezing tray and freeze till half set. Remove and beat with a whisk or electric beater until smooth. Doing so will help keep the texture of the ice cream soft and creamy while it sets..

3. Return to tray and freeze till well set.
